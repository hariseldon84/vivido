Style Model

The editor that learns how you cut

Every project teaches Vivido your cut rhythm, hook structure, and audio treatment. At project 5 it surfaces insights. At project 20, it pre-stages your rough cut. Zero creators who've completed 20+ projects have churned.

YouTube-Native

No more post-export YouTube Studio work. Mark chapters as first-class timeline objects. One-click Publish Package: main MP4 + Shorts + 3 thumbnail candidates + chapters.txt.
